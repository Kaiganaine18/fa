Dear Dad

Where do we even begin? You are the steady hand that guided us through every storm, the warm voice that calmed every fear, and the example we have tried — however imperfectly — to follow in everything we do.

We remember the early mornings you rose before the rest of us, the evenings you stayed up to make sure we got home safe, and every ordinary Tuesday in between that you showed up as our hero without ever asking for recognition.

You taught us that love is not just something you say — it is something you do, day after day, without keeping score. We have watched you work hard, laugh generously, forgive quickly, and love deeply, and we are so proud to call you ours.

On this special day, we want you to know: you are seen, you are celebrated, and you are loved beyond anything these words can hold.

With all our hearts, Your Family ♥
